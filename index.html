ABOUT US

WHAT WE DO?

FeintIQ teaches the fundamentals of boxing and trains aspiring champions. From beginners to pros, we focus on skill, discipline, and strategy to help every athlete reach their full potential.

OUR STORY

Founded in 1975 by former Olympian Peter Parker from New York, FeintIQ has trained over 150 world champions. With decades of experience and a deep passion for boxing, we continue to inspire and develop the next generation of champions.
